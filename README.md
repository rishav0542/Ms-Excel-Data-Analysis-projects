## Overview
This interactive Excel dashboard analyzes Myntra's social media performance across 300+ posts on Instagram, Facebook, X, and YouTube (2024-2025). It uncovers engagement trends, platform ROI, hashtag effectiveness, campaign uplift, and follower growth to optimize content strategy and boost interactions by 15-20%.

**Key Focus**: Turn raw metrics (likes, reach) into actionable insights for fashion e-commerce campaigns like "End of Season Sale" and "Fashion Upgrade." Total reach analyzed: 52.8M; Avg Engagement Rate (ER): 9.3%.

Explore sheets for pivots, charts, and formulasâ€”perfect for marketers or analysts.

## Features
- **Interactive Pivots**: Slice by platform, content type, or campaign.
- **KPI Calculations**: ER, uplift %, ROI (e.g., `=(During_ER - Before_ER)/Before_ER`).
- **Visuals**: Bar/line charts for trends; heatmaps for performance.
- **Campaign ROI Tracker**: Compares pre/during periods with spend efficiency.

## Data Overview
- **Posts**: 300 entries (Reels, Images, etc.) with metrics like 4K+ likes on top posts.
- **Platforms**: Instagram (ER leader: 19.3%), Facebook (reach: 13.8M).
- **Hashtags**: #StyleWithMyntra (17.2% ER); 34 uses analyzed.
- **Campaigns**: 4 major (e.g., $220K Festive Edit budget).
